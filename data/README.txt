Resume Photo Directory
=====================

Place your profile photo here with the following naming convention:

Format: [Your_Name]_image.[ext]

Examples:
- Bhuvanesh_S_image.jpg
- John_Doe_image.png
- Jane_Smith_image.jpeg

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png
Recommended size: 2.5cm x 3cm (passport-size)

Note: Your resume will be automatically named: [Your_Name]_Resume.pdf
